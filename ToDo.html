To Do

Here are some of the things that you should do in Singapore!

Here are some things to do :




Maxwell Food Centre

Eat like a local at the Maxwell Food Centre. It is Singapore's most iconic hawker centre in Chinatown which was started in the 1929. There are more than 100 available stalls. Enjoy local favourites including chicken rice, kaya toast, Laska, and chilli crab. Drink like a local! Try out Milo Dinosaur, and sugarcane juice and order coffee like a local.

Some of the MUST TRYs are Tian Tian Hainanese Chicken Rice,China Street Rickshaw Noodles and Hum Jin Pang


Damage $
Operating Hours 8am to 2am daily
avoid peak hours to avoid the crowd!
Address 1 Kadayanallur St, Singapore 069184
A 2-min walk from the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple and Museum







Jewel Changi Airport

Discover Jewel Changi Airport!  a top-notch entertainment and shopping complex with a natural theme that is connected to and linked to Changi Airport in Singapore. Its most iconic symbol is the Rain Vortex, which is also the world's tallest indoor waterfall. Not only is it a tourist attraction, but there are also lots of activities for all ages.
Fun Fact: It is also one of the largest indoor Garden in Singapore

Some of the activities that you can do there are the Walking Net,Mirror Maze and the Hedge Maze. Theres so much more that you can do there, such as visiting Pokemon Centre or chill at the Lounge


Damage $-$$$
Operating Hours 24 Hours
Address 78 Airport Blvd., Singapore 819666
Connected to the Arrival Hall (level 1) of Terminal 1.







Hi Roller Indoor Skating Rink

Want to get active while bonding with your loved one? HiRoller's the way to do it! HiRoller, is the largest indoor roller skating rink in Singapore. There are rinks where experts can perform stunts with ease and for beginners to have some laughs. You're transported back to the 1960s by the vibrant disco lights and upbeat music.


Damage $-$$
Operating Hours Mon-Thur 11am - 6.30pm
Fri 12pm - 10pm
Sat-Sun 11am - 12pm
Address 1 Pasir Ris Cl, Level 5 E!Hub, Singapore 519599







Big Bus Tours - Choice of 'City' or 'Heritage' Route

Hop aboard the open-top Big Bus to take in the breathtaking views from the open-top deck. From the comfort of your seat, explore and discover more about Singapore's various attractions and sights.Choose either Yellow 'City' or Red 'Heritage' Route, or do both!

Choose the Yellow 'City' Route if you want to see Singapore's landmarks such as Marina Bay Sands, Esplanade, and the National Gallery.

Choose the Red 'Heritage' Route to learn more about Singapore's rich Cultural where you're touring streets like Chinatown, Kampong glam, and little India.


Damage $$-$$$
Duration Approx. 60mins / route
Address BIG BUS & DUCK Tourist Hub, Suntec City Convention Centre #01-330
Info Access to 12-language audio commentary, including a kids channel!
